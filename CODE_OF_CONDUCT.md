# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Sapience AI Suite community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and apologizing

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassing or discriminatory comments or actions
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Insults or attacks on character, background, or beliefs
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Threats or intimidation

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces including:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Official community channels
- Public events and conferences
- Any official communications

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may be subject to:
- Warnings
- Temporary or permanent bans from the community
- Escalation to platform authorities if necessary

## Reporting

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct, please report it by:
1. Contacting the maintainers directly
2. Opening a private issue or discussion
3. Emailing the team at discovery.shariq.ali@sapienceai.co

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and with care.
